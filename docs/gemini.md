# Vision

Reddit Background Viewer aims to provide a serene and ever-changing visual experience by pulling high-quality images from Reddit and displaying them as a full-screen background. It's designed to be minimal, unobtrusive, and visually pleasing.

The project focuses on:
- **Simplicity**: No complex setup or configuration needed.
- **Beauty**: Bringing the best of Reddit's photography and art to your screen.
- **Accessibility**: Ensuring a smooth experience for all users.
